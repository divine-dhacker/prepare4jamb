How to Use This App

  1. Select Class Level: At the top of the page, choose the appropriate class level (e.g., P1, Junior Secondary) for your student. The AI will tailor the definitions and sentences to this level.
  2. Enter Words: In the left panel, use the text boxes to enter one or more words you want to include in the vocabulary assignment. Click the **"+ Add word"** button to add more input fields.
  3. Generate the Table: Once you have entered all your words, click the **"Create Table"** button. The AI will process the words and generate a table with a **Word**, **Meaning**, and **Sentence** for each.
  4. Review and Edit: The generated table will appear on the right. You can click on any cell in the **Meaning** or **Sentence** columns to edit the text if you need to make adjustments.
  5. Save and Share: Use the buttons above the table to manage your assignment:
    • **Download CSV**: Saves the table as a spreadsheet file.
    • **Copy**: Copies the table to your clipboard as a formatted text.
    • **Share**: Generates a unique link you can share with others.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI choose the meaning and sentence?

The AI generates the meaning and sentence based on the **class level** you select at the top. For younger classes (e.g., KG, P1), it provides very simple definitions and short, straightforward sentences. For older students (e.g., Senior Secondary), the content becomes more complex and contextually rich, suitable for their academic level.

Can I edit the generated table?

Yes, absolutely! The generated table is fully editable. You can click directly on any cell in the **Meaning** or **Sentence** columns and type to make corrections or personalize the content. The changes will be saved automatically within your browser session.

Is my data saved or shared?

Your data is not saved on our servers unless you explicitly click the **Share** button. The share feature uses a secure link to store the table data temporarily so it can be viewed by others. If you don't use the share feature, all data remains in your browser and is not sent anywhere.

What do the navigation buttons at the bottom do?

The buttons at the bottom help you navigate the app:
  • **Home**: Takes you to the main page of the application.
  • **My List**: Directs you to a section where you can manage your personal vocabulary lists.
  • **Help**: Leads you to the privacy policy and help page.
